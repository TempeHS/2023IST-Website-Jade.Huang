Capybaras

All About Capybaras!

Capybaras are very cute and furry. They are brown with a large body and a perpetually calm aura that makes it a friend of all animals.

Capybaras have been seen to hang out (real) and ride crocodiles (it was actually another capybara but shhh).

Very cute video of capybara sniffing a dog and the dog sniffing it back: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SCwcJsBYL3o

Diet 😋

Capybaras are vegetarians (caring for their fellow animals) so their diet consists of grasses, barks, tubers, and sugar cane.

Capybaras can swim, so they can eat aquatic plants.

Capybaras getting scratched and stroked in a pool: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zF0Z6NDZp-k

Capybaras swimming very happily: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qGTsPrhNtIY

Lots of adorable footage of capybaras eating whole watermelons have been filmed, but they should not eat too much sugar (that includes all fruit!) as they cannot process sugar and it can lead to bloating 🤒.
